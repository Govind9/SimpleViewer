Rockville Winter 2017 Heat Sheet:

Hello Competitor! Herein, you will find a list of names and heats per event. Simply find your name in the event which you are registered for, and remember your heat number. When we call your number, please bring your puzzle to the appropriate scrambling table and wait for your name to be called to compete.

If you miss your heat, that’s okay, because we will have a last call.

If you don’t see your scorecard, come back during last call.

If you miss last call, don’t worry, we’re nice and we have a second last call just to be safe!
